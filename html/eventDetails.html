DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION - DATA DRIVEN ORGANISATIONS

DATE: JULY 17, 2021

TIME: 2:00PM - 3:30PM

Share this event:

ABOUT EVENT

Digitalization and deep digital competencies are necessary for organizations to survive. Yet digital talent is scarce in Malaysia, posing a challenge to transformation. Discover what can be done to develop a talent pipeline in data and digital skills.

WHY ATTEND?

  • Learn about the benefits data science can bring to organizations.
  • Get expert advise on how decision-makers can create a data-driven culture in organizations.
  • Learn how to recruit and develop relevant analytics talent.
WHAT IT WILL COVER

  • How Data Science helps companies
  • How does a company embark on a data-driven journey?
  • How do companies develop a data talent pipeline?
